Curated List of Math Blogs and Other Math Resources

Blogs

  • Terry Tao's Blog - I don't understand a thing he writes about mathematically, but he gives good advice.
  • Math3ma, Tai Danae Bradley's Blog - Bradley is a grad student in New York and writes good posts (particularly ones about Category Theory) that aim to connect rigour with intuition.
  • AMS PhD+Epsilon - An AMS blog run by early career mathematicians in the US.
  • AMS Grad Student Blog - An AMS blog run by graduate students.
  • The n-Category Cafe - A good blog to get news about the world of Category Theory.

Publications

  • Quanta Magazine - A magazine about mathematics, computer science, biology and physics.
  • Inference Review - Not quite sure what this is yet, but I've already read three articles and they were all interesting.
  • Notices of the AMS - I haven't noticed it before, but I've often stumbled upon a pdf that is from an issue of AMS Notices.

Forum

  • Mathoverflow - I particularly like their soft questions. Useful for resource requests.
  • Everything About X- Just found it. This is kinda cool.
